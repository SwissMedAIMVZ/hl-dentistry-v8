Usage Rules

  • ✓Keep clearspace around the mark equal to the width of the HL strokes
  • ✓Place on white, surface grey, or the brand navy background
  • ✓Use SVG at any size — renders crisp on all devices
  • ✓Maintain the signal green dot in every context
  • ✕Don't stretch, skew, or rotate the mark
  • ✕Don't change stroke weights or letter proportions
  • ✕Don't place on busy photographs without a solid backdrop
  • ✕Don't recolor outside the palette above
